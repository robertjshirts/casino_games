BLACKJACK RULES

Card Values:

                - Number cards (2-10) are worth their face value.
                - Face cards (Jack, Queen, King) are each worth 10.
                - Aces can be worth 1 or 11, depending on which value benefits the hand more.

Dealing:

                - Each player, including the dealer, is initially dealt two cards.
                - Players' cards are usually dealt face up, while the dealer has one card face up 
                    (known as the "upcard") and one face down (the "hole card").
                - If a player is dealt an Ace and a 10-value card (10, Jack, Queen, King) as their initial two cards, 
                    they have a "Blackjack" or "Natural."

Player's Turn:

                Players can choose from the following actions:
                - Hit: Take an additional card to increase the hand value.
                - Stand: Keep the current hand without taking additional cards.
                - Double Down: Double the initial bet and take exactly one more card. In some casinos, 
                    you may be allowed to double down on any two cards, while in others, it may be restricted to certain hand values.
                - Split: If the initial two cards are of the same rank, the player can split them into two separate hands, 
                    each with its own bet. Additional cards are then dealt to each new hand.

Dealer's Turn:

                - The dealer reveals the hole card.
                - The dealer must hit until their hand totals 17 or higher.
                - Some casinos have specific rules for the dealer, such as hitting on a soft 17 
                    (a hand containing an Ace valued as 11).

Winning/Losing:

                - Players win if their hand is closer to 21 than the dealer's hand without going over 21.
                - If a player busts (goes over 21), they lose their bet.
                - If the dealer busts and the player hasn't, the player wins.
                - Ties result in a "push," and the player's bet is returned.
